On-Going Projects

Maynilad Quezon City Business Area Office

Maynilad Water Services, Inc. is a water and wastewater services provider in the West Zone of the Greater Manila Area. It is a subsidiary of Metro Pacific Investments Corporation (MPIC) and DMCI Holdings, Inc.

The Maynilad Water Services office located at Quezon City is a project of KEDC, which stands for Kennedy Energy and Development Corp. KEDC is a company that provides engineering and design services for various infrastructure projects in the Philippines.


DPWH Baliuag Business Center

The Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) Baliuag Business Center is a project of the Kennedy Energy and Development Corp. (KEDC) which aims to promote economic growth in the region.

This project is situated in the heart of Baliuag, Bulacan and is strategically located near commercial establishments which makes it an ideal location for businesses.


DPWH Quezon City New Haven Business Center

This project is a significant development initiative that aims to transform an area in Quezon City into a thriving business district. The project offers numerous benefits to the local community, including the creation of job opportunities, increased economic activity, and improved quality of life.

The project is committed to sustainability and minimizing its environmental impact, and encourages partnerships and collaboration between government agencies, private companies, and the local community.


Maynilad EMF Project - Las Piñas

KEDC, or the Kennedy Energy and Development Corp., has on-Going project in Las Piñas that involved the installation of electro magnetic flow meters using insertion tapping. The project aimed to improve the efficiency and accuracy of the city's water distribution system.

The use of magmeters allowed for real-time monitoring of the water flow and quality, enabling the city to quickly detect and address any issues. The project also resulted in reduced water loss and improved revenue collection for the city.


Maynilad EMF Project - Manila

Electro Magnetic Flow Meter (EMF) is a device used for measuring the flow rate of liquids in closed pipelines.

The Manila Project by KEDC aims to install EMFM in various locations across the city to accurately measure the flow rate of water in the pipelines and prevent wastage and leakage.


Indang Gymnasium

Indang Gymnasium is a project of the Kennedy Energy and Development Corp. (KEDC) that aims to provide a world-class sports facility for the people of Indang, Philippines.

The gymnasium features state-of-the-art equipment and facilities, including basketball courts, volleyball courts, badminton courts, a fitness center, and a swimming pool. It also has a seating capacity of up to 3,000 people, making it suitable for large-scale events.


DPWH Gulod Roadnetwork and Pipelaying

The Gulod Roadnetwork and Pipelaying project is a significant undertaking that aims to provide better access to basic services such as water and electricity in the area. This project involves the construction of new roads and the laying of pipelines to connect households to the main water and power sources.

The Gulod Roadnetwork and Pipelaying project is crucial to the development of the area. With improved access to basic services, residents will be able to enjoy a better quality of life. The new roads will also make it easier for people to travel and transport goods, which can boost economic activity in the region.
