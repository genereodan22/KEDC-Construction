About us

Kennedy Energy and Development Corp. commenced its operations in June 2015 with the motivation to provide solutions and infrastructures that will augment daily Filipino living. The company is comprised of a team of knowledgeable and skilled technopreneurs who aim to respond to the increasing demand for quality construction service providers and cutting-edge technology for sourcing sustainable energy.

Initially started with a focus on reliable and sustainable technologies, we have expanded our services to include general contracting services for construction of roads, buildings, and other infrastructures in 2018. As we thrive and continue to be customer-centric, our company continues to provide the highest standard of services that are tailorfit for each customer. Quality workmanship and dedication are at the heart of our company’s passion for excellence while maintaining a positive and professional outlook in the conduct of business.

MISSION

"To serve as a bridge way for Filipino homes and businesses to have access to cost-efficient, innovative, and exceptional renewable energy and infrastructure for everyday living."

VISION

"To be a recognized and at the same time trusted company committed to providing the best possible and most efficient construction and energy solutions to communities."
