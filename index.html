Commercial Construction Projects

KEDC is a commercial contractor that has been providing top-notch services to businesses for over 5 years. KEDC specialize in construction, renovation, and maintenance of commercial buildings. Our team consists of highly skilled professionals who are dedicated to delivering quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Residential construction projects

KEDC is a new residential contractor that has recently entered the market with a fresh perspective and innovative ideas. With a team of experienced professionals, KEDC aims to provide high-quality construction services to its clients. The company's focus on sustainability and energy efficiency sets it apart from other contractors in the industry. KEDC uses advanced technology and eco-friendly materials to create homes that are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible.

Infrastructure construction projects

KEDC offers a wide range of transportation infrastructure services, including road construction, bridge construction, and railway construction. The company's expertise in this field is unmatched, and aims to be one of the successful infrastructure contractor.

Design and Build
